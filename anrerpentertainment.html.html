[ Help Center ]

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Anrerp Entertainment. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

Q1. What is Anrerp Entertainment and what makes you different?
⌄
Anrerp Entertainment specializes in delivering end-to-end entertainment and event solutions, combining creative planning with technical execution. Our approach focuses on customized event experiences, a strong vendor and artist network, high-quality production standards, and transparent pricing and communication. We transform concepts into professionally executed, memorable experiences.
Q2. What types of events do you manage?
⌄
We handle a wide spectrum including: Entertainment Events (DJ Nights, Club Events, Live Concerts, Music Festivals, Celebrity Appearances), Corporate Events (Product Launches, Conferences & Seminars, Award Functions), Institutional Events (College Fests, Cultural Programs, Farewell/Welcome Events), and Social Events (Weddings & Receptions, Private Parties, Birthday Celebrations, Customised Parties).
Q3. Do you provide complete event solutions?
⌄
Yes, we provide turnkey solutions including concept development, budget planning, vendor sourcing, stage/sound/lighting, artist booking & management, and full event execution & coordination. Clients may also opt for partial services only.
Q4. How does the booking process work?
⌄
The process follows five steps: (1) Initial consultation and requirement discussion, (2) Proposal and quotation submission, (3) Agreement signing, (4) Advance payment for booking confirmation, (5) Planning and full event execution.
Q5. How far in advance should I book?
⌄
We recommend a minimum of 2–4 weeks for small events, 1–2 months for medium events, and 2–6 months for large-scale events. Early booking ensures better availability of artists and vendors.
Q6. Can you help with artist booking?
⌄
Yes, we specialize in artist management including DJs (local and international), singers and bands, celebrity artists and influencers, and hosts/anchors. Artist fees vary based on popularity, demand, and availability.
Q7. What is a Technical Rider?
⌄
A technical rider is a document provided by artists specifying sound requirements, lighting setup, stage specifications, and hospitality requirements. We ensure all technical riders are fulfilled to maintain performance quality.
Q8. Do you assist with permissions and licenses?
⌄
Yes, we guide and assist in obtaining police permissions, sound/noise permits, fire safety approvals, and venue permissions. Final responsibility may depend on the contractual agreement between both parties.
Q9. What factors affect event pricing?
⌄
Pricing depends on event scale and duration, venue location, equipment and production needs, artist and talent fees, and logistics and manpower requirements. Every event is unique and is quoted accordingly.
Q10. Do you offer customized packages?
⌄
Yes, every event is customizable based on budget, theme, target audience, and client vision. There are no rigid package structures — we build around your needs.
Q11. What happens in case of delays or emergencies?
⌄
We have contingency plans for technical failures, artist delays, and weather disruptions. External factors beyond our reasonable control fall under force majeure conditions as outlined in our Terms & Conditions.
Q12. Do you travel for events?
⌄
Yes, we operate across multiple cities and regions. Travel, accommodation, and logistics costs are included transparently in the final quotation.
Q13. Do you provide security and crowd management?
⌄
Yes, upon request we can arrange professional security personnel, bouncers, and crowd control systems to ensure a safe event environment.
Q14. Can the event be rescheduled?
⌄
Yes, subject to availability, vendor/artist policies, and any applicable additional costs. Rescheduling requests must be communicated in writing as early as possible.
Q15. Do you provide post-event services?
⌄
Yes, our post-event services include comprehensive event reports, media coverage coordination, and photo and video delivery as agreed in the contract.

Terms & Conditions

Please read these Terms & Conditions carefully before engaging Anrerp Entertainment for any event services. By proceeding with a booking, you confirm your acceptance of all clauses below.

Last updated: January 2025  |  Jurisdiction: Hyderabad, Telangana, India

CLAUSE 1
Definitions

"Company" refers to Anrerp Entertainment, a registered event management entity operating under applicable laws.

"Client" refers to any individual, group, or organization that engages the Company for event services.

"Event" refers to the specific function, show, or service agreed upon between the Company and the Client.

CLAUSE 2
Booking & Confirmation

All bookings are formally confirmed only upon receipt of a duly signed agreement and the advance payment as stipulated in the quotation. Verbal confirmations, WhatsApp messages, or informal communications shall not constitute a binding booking.

CLAUSE 3
Payment Terms
Standard Schedule
  • Fifty percent (50%) of the total invoice amount is payable as advance at the time of booking confirmation.
  • The remaining fifty percent (50%) balance is due and payable before the commencement of event execution.
  • Any additional charges arising during the event must be settled immediately upon invoicing.
Late Payment

Failure to meet payment deadlines may result in suspension of services, cancellation of the booking without liability to the Company, and forfeiture of amounts already paid.

CLAUSE 4
Pricing & Quotations

All quotations issued by the Company are valid for a period of seven (7) to fifteen (15) calendar days from the date of issue, unless otherwise specified in writing.

The Company reserves the right to revise pricing due to vendor cost fluctuations, artist fee changes, or external economic factors beyond its control. Clients will be notified of any such changes prior to a revised agreement being issued.

CLAUSE 5
Cancellation Policy
Cancellation Charges
  • Cancellation more than 30 days before the event date: Partial refund after deduction of all non-recoverable third-party costs and administrative charges.
  • Cancellation between 15 and 30 days before the event: 50% of the total invoice amount is retained by the Company.
  • Cancellation less than 15 days before the event: 100% of the total invoice amount is retained by the Company. No refund shall be issued.
Non-Refundable Components

Regardless of cancellation timing, the following components are strictly non-refundable: artist booking fees and advances, vendor advances and deposits, logistics and transportation arrangements, and any third-party permits or licenses obtained.
